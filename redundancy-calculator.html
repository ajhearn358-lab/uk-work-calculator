About This UK Redundancy Calculator

This calculator provides an estimate of statutory redundancy pay and minimum notice entitlement based on current UK statutory rules. It is designed to help employees understand what they may be entitled to if facing redundancy.

How Statutory Redundancy Pay Is Calculated

Statutory redundancy pay depends on:

  • Your age
  • Your length of continuous service
  • Your weekly pay (subject to a statutory cap)

The formula applies:

  • 0.5 week’s pay for each full year under age 22
  • 1 week’s pay for each full year aged 22–40
  • 1.5 weeks’ pay for each full year aged 41 and over

Notice Period Entitlement

Employees are generally entitled to at least one week’s notice for each full year of service, up to a maximum of 12 weeks, unless their contract provides more.
